## Brotato Clone

A polished top-down twin-stick shooter inspired by Brotato.

### How to Play

Open `index.html` in a browser.

- **WASD** — Move your potato
- **Weapons fire automatically** at the nearest enemy
- Survive each wave (30 seconds), then visit the **Shop** to buy weapons and upgrades
- Collect money drops from defeated enemies
- Boss fights every 5 waves

### Features

- **10 weapons**: Pistol, Rifle, Shotgun, Rocket Launcher, Crossbow, Uzi, Minigun, Sniper, Grenade Launcher, Plasma Gun
- **6 upgrades**: Max HP, Speed, Damage, Regen, Magnet, Crit Chance
- **5 enemy types**: Basic, Fast, Big, Tank, Boss
- Wave-scaling difficulty
- Particle effects, screen shake, damage numbers
- Procedural sound effects
- Shop system between waves with buy/sell
